Postnatal contacts are recommended for healthy women & newborns between 48 and 72 hours, between seven and 14 days, and during week six after birth.

Worldwide, more than 3 in 10 women & babies do not currently receive postnatal care in the first days after birth - the period when most maternal and infant deaths occur.
The birth of a baby is a life-changing moment, one that is bound by love, hope and excitement, but it can also cause unprecedented stress & anxiety.
Parents need strong health care & support systems, especially women, whose needs are too often neglected when the baby comes.
